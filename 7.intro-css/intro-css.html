Bangtan Discography

School Trilogy

2 Cool 4 Skool

1st single album

1st overall album from BTS, it defines it's entrance to the music industry talking about their hopes and dreams about becoming big artists, follow their desired paths and ignoring society pressure to give up their real selves. Establishing their style and making their voices be heard.

Physical Copies Sold: 420k (Gaon Chart)

O!RUL8,2?

1st mini album

With their 2nd album of this trilogy BTS offers the message that “you must find your happiness and your own life before it’s too late.”
Lyrics that brutally lay open reality as it is deliver a bold admonition to a world that inflicts despair. Children are raised as studying machines without a chance to think about the future. A world where classmates are not friends but competitors trying to trample each other. BTS sings about the wounds and anguish that such hopeless life has left on today’s teens...

Physical Copies Sold: 412k (Gaon Chart)

Skool Luv Affair

2nd mini album

Focusing on school and teens. The album’s main theme is the love that blossoms in the school. This album is a culmination of the “School Trilogy” project that discusses the topics of most interest to teens their own age—dreams, happiness and love. Such penetrating insight into the hearts and minds of young people led to the absolute support that BTS has received from fans their age.

Physical Copies Sold: 1.2m (Gaon Chart)

Transition to Adulthood

Dark & Wild

1st studio album

The boys are no longer in their school uniforms. They have become tougher, singing that their love is not to be treated lightly. A year and two months into their debut, BTS made sure that the visuals, performance and all the elements of their first studio album are flawless. Tracks that highlight the different genres of hip hop—South, West, Boom Bap and Trap—provide a clear direction of BTS' music.

Physical Copies Sold: 650k (Gaon Chart)
